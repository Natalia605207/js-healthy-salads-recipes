Power Salad with Chicken

Cooking time: 20 mins

20 : 00

Ingredients

expand_more
  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, grated
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground pepper
  • 4 cups torn green-leaf lettuce
  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup halved grape tomatoes
  • 1 cup halved and sliced cucumber
  • ½ cup slivered red onion
  • ⅓ cup sliced pepperoncini
  • ⅓ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons toasted unsalted sunflower seeds

Instructions

expand_more
  1. Whisk oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, sugar, salt and pepper together in a large bowl.
  2. Add lettuce, spinach, chicken, tomatoes, cucumber, onion and pepperoncini. Toss to coat.
  3. Serve sprinkled with feta and sunflower seeds.
